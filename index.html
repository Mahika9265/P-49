Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • The chameleons' eyes can rotate and focus separately on 180-degree arcs, so they can see two different objects at the same time.
  • Chameleons actual eyesight is great they can see small insects 5-10 meters away.

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • They can fly at the altitude of 6000 feet.
  • Pigeons can fly at the speed of 50 to 60 miles per hour.
